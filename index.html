About Sanjana Rathi

Sanjana is a techie, social-entrepreneur, artist and traveller, who recently graduated from the London School of Economics and Political Science(LSE) with a Msc. degree in Managemnt of Information Systems and Digital Innovation. Altough she is born in West Bengal, in a family native to Rajasthan and raised in Karnataka, she identifies herself culturally as a Bharatiya. Determined to be involved in taking action for a better world, this website exhibits her work and zest for life!
